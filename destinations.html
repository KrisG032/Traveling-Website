Destination Overview

Daily Itinerary

Days 1-3: Santorini

  • Arrival and luxury hotel check-in
  • Oia sunset photography tour
  • Caldera catamaran cruise
  • Wine tasting at local vineyards

Days 4-6: Mykonos

  • Paradise and Super Paradise beaches
  • Little Venice walking tour
  • Water sports activities
  • Sunset cocktail experience

Days 7-10: Crete

  • Knossos Palace guided tour
  • Traditional cooking class
  • Samaria Gorge hike

  • Daily breakfast
  • Professional guides
